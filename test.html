Fellow Binancians,
Binance Futures will launch USDT-margined CTSI perpetual contracts with up to 25x leverage at 2021-10-26 03:30 AM (UTC).
Please note: CTSI perpetual contracts are USDT-margined futures contracts that use USDT as collateral. Users will be able to select between 1-25x leverage.
